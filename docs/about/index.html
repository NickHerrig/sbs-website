About Me

My new is Andrew Dailey and I’m an independent programmer from Des Moines, Iowa. I have multiple years of industry experience working a variety of jobs from Linux system administration to Python-based ETL to large-scale system automation. Programming is my primary hobby and I tend to focus on high-performance, systems software.

I am proficient in Python and C and am currently honing my skills with Go and Rust. When it comes to writing C, I’ve spent plenty of time researching and testing the most effective way to achieve simple, cross-platform application builds. Most of the Makefiles for the C projects on my GitHub reflect this work. I also enjoy graphics-programming when I find the time: both rasterization and ray-tracing.
